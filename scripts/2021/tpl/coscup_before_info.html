Hi {{name}},

COSCUP x RubyConfTW 2021 即將在 07/31 - 08/01 線上舉辦！

在即將到來的活動有幾件事情需要事先準備的，在此一一說明，讓當日的活動可以順利進行。

活動前

議程表

還沒看這次有哪些議題的，可以先看看已公告的議程表，每一軌的的議程表上方將會在議程開始時出現 YouTube 轉播。讓妳有隨意逛教室、逛議程的感受。

講者須知

如果您是講者，在活動前應該會收到關於 StreamYard 的連結邀請，如果沒有收到，請聯絡您的社群負責人或是議程主持人。

線上會場大廳

由於目前此刻，我們的線上會場還在加緊趕工敲敲打打中，建議連回到 COSCUP 官網首頁，由首頁上的指引進入。

我們這次會使用 Gather.Town 平台登入，在活動開始期間，可以先閱讀操作手冊！

  • 攤位區 01：連結
  • 攤位區 02：連結
  • BoF：連結
  • 前夜派對（Welcome Party！）：連結

個人贊助

感謝各方好友支持 COSCUP！每贊助一筆 2500 元，可獲得 COSCUP 2021 獨家贈品一份。或是其他金額小額捐款/香油錢（無贈品）。個人贊助由此去！

活動前一天 7/30

前夜派對

依照往年，活動前一天的晚上我們會舉行「前夜派對」！這個活動不論隔天是否要參加 COSCUP、只要你有空，都可以來這裡和好友、社群夥伴喝一杯，有可能會遇到心目中期待已久的大神講者！

（喝一杯這件事可能要啟動邊緣運算（Edge computing），建議各位可以備足點心、零食或是啤酒在電腦前和大家喝一杯！線上飲み会！）

如何前往「前夜派對」會場，請從「會場大廳」地圖、位於右上方的入口處進入（Party Entrance）。

活動日 7/31 - 8/01

如果你一早起來不知道下一步該如何開始，可以先連到 COSCUP 官網，看看有無最新消息發布！接著可以開始逛「議程表」。

這次的活動因為轉為線上，我們沒有現場的攝影紀錄組協助，但如果你在活動期間發現不錯的畫面，在分享的時候也請幫忙加上 #COSCUP #COSCUP2021 #COSCUPxRubyConfTW2021 的標籤，感謝！

大會主議程軌

大會主議程會在 08:45 開始，最後一場主議程是 16:10 - 17:10。

社群議程軌

各社群議程軌會在 10:00 開始，於每日的 16:00 結束。社群議程結束後請記得再回到「大會主議程軌」！

大會攤位區

這次活動我們還是有把攤位區的場地搬到線上喔！不論是贊助商攤位或是社群攤位，歡迎在活動期間到攤位區去逛逛，當然也有大地遊戲可以闖關！

入口處在請從「會場大廳」地圖、位於左上方的入口處進入（Booth Entrance）

7/31、8/1 12:10 - 13:00＠Gather Town Hiring Room.

其他溫馨提醒：進入房間請關閉麥克風。

逛攤位！獲得限量 2021 獨家贈品！

  • 注意：除了「天瓏書局」沒有參與攤位大地遊戲，其他 20 個攤位（贊助商 / 社群）都有通關密語要收歐。
  • 注意 2：你沒有看錯，贈品在 COSCUP2022 實體活動發送。

徵文活動

今年首次舉辦的徵文活動，邀請各位參與者寫下聽議程或是參與大會的心得發布在網路上，只要回報文章連結並且符合徵文規範，就可以獲得去年限量製作的開源酒杯或是線材包喔！

閃電秀 / Lightning Talk

每個人 5 分鐘，時間一到就插播一隻可愛的貓貓給大家看 🤡，歡迎將您想曝光的 Projects、Idea 或小議題在這裡跟大家分享吧！

If you haven't seen what topics are on the agenda yet, you can check out the announced agenda first. The YouTube broadcast will appear at the top of the agenda for each track. This will give you the feeling of walking through the classroom and listening to talks at your leisure.

Jul 30 - The Day Before the Event

Welcome Party

As tradition goes, we will hold a "Welcome Party" the night before the event! No matter if you are attending COSCUP or not, as long as you are free, you can come to have a drink with your friends and community partners. Who knows - you may even meet the speaker you have been waiting for!

(The drink may require activating edge computing, so we suggest you bring enough snacks or beers to have a drink with everyone in front of the computer! Online drinking sesssion! 線上飲み会! Virtual kanpai!)

Please enter through the "Party Entrance" located at the top right of the "Conference Hall" map to arrive at the "Welcome Party" venue.

Jul 31 - Aug 1

Get comfy, let’s have some fun!

Go to the COSCUP official website to see if there is any news released. Then you can start browsing the "Agenda".

This year, we do not have the on-site photography team because we switched to online. We want YOU to be our photographers and share your favourite moments or great images during the two days event. Use #COSCUP #COSCUP2021 #COSCUPxRubyConfTW2021 to spread the word!

Main Track

The main track will start at 8:45 and the last talk from the main track will be 16:10 - 17:10.

Community Track

Each community track will start at 10:00 and end at 16:00 each day. Please remember to return to the Main Track after the end of Community Track.

Visit the Booth! (Get a limited COSCUP 2021 exclusive gift)

The epidemic has made our event difficult, but we can't lose the tradition! We have moved the booth online. Whether it's a sponsor booth or a community booth, you're welcome to visit them during the event. And of course, there will also be games to play!

Collect the passwords of the sponsor booths/community booths. Submit the correct answers, and we will store the giveaway points in the registered email address. Then, you can bring the OPass app to us to claim the exclusive gift during the COSCUP 2022 event.

Note:

  • Except for "Tenlong Computer Bookstore", which is not participating in the game, all other 20 booths (sponsors/communities) will have passwords to collect.
  • You read it right, the gift will be given out at the COSCUP 2022 physical event.

The entrance is located at the Booth Entrance on the top left side of the Conference Hall.
