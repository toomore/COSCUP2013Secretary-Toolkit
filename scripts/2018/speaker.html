How to get the badge of the conference? / 如何報到？

We use a special (and open sourced) app “COSCUP Pass.” There are various things you can do with the app, including the check-in procedure.

  1. Please use the unique link to download it: Download Link
  2. Currently we support iOS and Android, if your don't have the devices we supported, just use the QRCode attached in this mail at the Manual Check-in Desk.

Please make sure that you install and login the app properly in advance before arriving the venue. If not, please click the unique link again.

（今年我們使用了 COSCUP Pass 這個 App 作為報到之用，請點選上方專屬於您的特殊連結下載 Android / iOS App 以便報到。無法下載者，可用本信隨付之 QRCode 於當天在人工報到櫃台報到。）

After you arrive the venue, you will use the App to check-in and get the welcome kit / your conference badge. Please follow the instructions of the document: Document Link

（請參考上述連結了解會場報到方式）

Please wear your badge during the conference.

Wifi?

In each room we have ethernet cable for the speaker, or you may want to use NTUST's wifi -- tell your room coordinator to get a guest account.

（每間講廳的講桌都有網路線可用，或可洽詢該議程軌的協調人，取得台科大的無線網路訪客帳號。）

Interview!
