主要資訊

  • 大會活動時間為 2020-08-01 - 2020-08-02

  • 如果...

    • 我是會眾，可以先看看行程上第一個議程教室在哪。
    • 我有帶小朋友，可以前往 TR515 遊樂園🎠

大會期間

  • 逛逛贊助商攤位(TR 2F)、社群攤位(TR 3F)、使用 OPass app，秀出 QRcode 讓攤位人員掃描取得點數，並到大會攤位(TR 3F)兌換贈品，數量有限！

Hi {{name}},

COSCUP 2020 is coming up soon. We have designed a guide for you so that you won’t miss every detail of COSCUP!

Main Information

  • Date: 2020-08-01 - 2020-08-02
  • Venue: National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (No. 43, Section 4, Keelung Road, Daan District, Taipei City)

Before the Conference

  • Donate to COSCUP: Donate to get COSCUP 2020 travel cable organizer bag or T-shirt
  • 7/31 Welcome Party(Own expense): Free entry. Drink a beer and have fun with FLOSS folks!
  • Fill out Personal Health Declaration Form, read Epidemic Prevention Guideline: Protect yourself & others
  • Pre-register BoF meet-up: So your community can find you!
  • As a parent, don’t forget to register COSCUP Kids service

Before Departure

  • Find out the best route to the venue (National Taiwan University of Science and Technology)
  • Agenda: Arrange your favorite sessions
  • Check out the venue map, so you won’t get lost！

Arriving at the Venue

  • Have you filled out the Personal Health Declaration Form?
  • Have you read the Epidemic Prevention Guideline?
  • For the health and safety of all participants, EVERYONE HAS TO WEAR SURGICAL MASKS AT ALL TIME during the conferences. 😷
  • Both Building TR and Building RB has registration tables and FREE welcome kits for participants
  • And we will take pictures of the event on the COSCUP. If you do not want to be taken photo, we will provide the red badge lanyard for you on Check-In desk.
  • If You Are…
    • A Speaker: You should head directly to your classroom.
    • A Participant: You can find the classroom of the first session from the agenda.
    • A Parent with kid(s): You can bring your kid(s) to TR515 Playground.
    • Bringing luggage: You can bring your luggage to TR517 to use luggage storage service.

During the Conference

  • Visit sponsors(TR 2F) and community booths(TR 3F) to collect points using OPass app. Show your QR code from OPass app to collect points. Redeem your points for gifts at the conference booth(TR 3F). These gifts are limited quantities and when they're gone, they're gone!
  • Visit “Volunteer Tasks” and collect rewards. Or come to the Volunteer Service Desk(TR 2F) to register as a volunteer of COSCUP 2021!
  • Go to BoF meeting rooms(TR 4F) to find your gathering. Share new findings with fellow FLOSS folks.
  • There will be a small dining car(TR 1F). You can order a cup of nitro cold brew(NCB) coffee with the beef ribs cooked by private chefs.
